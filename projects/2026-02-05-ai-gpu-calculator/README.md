## 💡 Why This Matters

Training modern AI models can quickly accumulate thousands of dollars; the AI GPU & Training Cost Calculator turns hardware choices into concrete budgets by using real GPU specs to compare A100, H100, and TPU options and estimate power consumption. Making trade-offs visible — cost per training run, throughput, and energy use — helps learners build practical intuition about model scaling and infrastructure. That clarity leads to smarter experiments, more realistic project planning, and better-aligned learning outcomes for anyone studying AI.
