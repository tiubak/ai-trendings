## 💡 Why This Matters

AI Mystic Oracle shows how generative language models, speech synthesis, and stateful interaction can be combined to create engaging, culturally informed experiences that feel personal and theatrical. Studying it exposes learners to prompt engineering, multimodal output, dialogue management, and the practical trade-offs around transparency, bias, and user expectations that arise when AI steps into human-like roles. As a playful but practical sandbox, it helps people learn how to design and evaluate systems that balance creativity, usability, and trust.
