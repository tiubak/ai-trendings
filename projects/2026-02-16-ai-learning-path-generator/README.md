## 💡 Why This Matters

An AI Learning Path Generator turns overwhelming choices into a clear, personalized roadmap, matching resources and milestones to your background and goals. With curated materials, stepped milestones, and time estimates, the tool makes learning measurable, efficient, and easier to sustain.
