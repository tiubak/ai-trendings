# Agentic Sandbox

> Design, probe, and perturb a tiny agentic LLM: pick a scenario, choose reward signals, and watch a simulated episode with risk annotations and alignment suggestions.

🔥 **Trending:** Inspired by recent coverage of Biomni-R0 (new agentic LLMs trained end-to-end with reinforcement learning) and discussions about Large Reasoning Models' illusion of thinking.
