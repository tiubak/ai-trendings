## 💡 Why This Matters

AI Stand-up Comedian demonstrates how language models and HuggingFace TTS combine to craft timing, tone, and persona, turning NLP concepts into performative outputs. Working on joke generation and voice narration gives learners practical experience with prompt engineering, style transfer, prosody control, and evaluation challenges like measuring humor and managing sensitive content. The project also surfaces real-world constraints around data curation, bias mitigation, and user-facing UX while offering a playful, low-stakes environment to experiment with multimodal AI techniques.
