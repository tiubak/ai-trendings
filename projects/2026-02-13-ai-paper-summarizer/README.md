## 💡 Why This Matters

The AI Research Paper Summarizer turns dense, jargon-heavy papers into plain-English takeaways so learners can absorb core ideas without getting stuck on terminology. By highlighting key findings, methods, and practical impact, it helps students prioritize what to read next and where to focus hands-on practice. This accelerates learning, makes advanced concepts more approachable, and reduces the chance of missing critical limitations or assumptions. For anyone building intuition in AI, that clarity saves time and guides smarter experimentation and critique.
