## 💡 Why This Matters

An AI Excuse Generator offers a compact, hands-on way to explore how language models handle creativity, tone, and constraint — showing how prompts steer specificity, plausibility and style. It also highlights practical challenges like hallucinations, bias, and the ethical limits of generated content, prompting consideration of safety measures and guardrails. As a case study in prompt engineering, evaluation and user experience, it helps learners translate abstract model behavior into concrete design and policy decisions.
