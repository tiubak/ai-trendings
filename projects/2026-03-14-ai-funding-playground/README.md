# AI Funding Playground

> Enter a one-line AI startup idea and get a playful, realistic simulated valuation, investor personas, and a mock term-sheet with negotiation playbook.

🔥 **Trending:** Inspired by recent AI funding headlines (e.g., teen-founded Aaru hitting a $1B valuation, Yann LeCun's $1B seed, and several $100M+ AI raises).


## 📊 Trending Analysis

Recent headlines show huge, rapid funding flows and headline valuations in the AI space, exposing founders to complex valuation and negotiation dynamics. This project responds by letting learners experiment with realistic funding scenarios, see the impact on cap tables, and practice negotiating term-sheet trade-offs in a low-risk, interactive way.
