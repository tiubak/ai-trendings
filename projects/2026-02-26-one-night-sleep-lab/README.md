## 💡 Why This Matters

One-Night Sleep Lab gives learners a hands-on way to see how AI can extract long-term health signals from a single night of sleep, connecting model-building to potential clinical impact. By working through lessons, mini-projects, and a model-design assistant, students confront real challenges—data quality, bias, interpretability, and privacy—that shape trustworthy AI. The experience sharpens practical skills in feature design, evaluation, and communicating uncertainty while encouraging thoughtful choices that matter when algorithms influence health decisions.
