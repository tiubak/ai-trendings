## 💡 Why This Matters

AI Mystic Fortune Teller offers a playful, hands-on way to explore how generative models create personalized text and images, showing learners how prompts, conditioning, and stylistic choices shape outputs. By experimenting with categories like love, career, wealth, and adventure, students can observe real issues such as personalization, bias, uncertainty, and the balance between creativity and accuracy. The project also surfaces practical concerns—interface design, user trust, and data privacy—that come up when AI meets people. This combination of creative application and technical insight helps bridge theory with real-world AI practice.
