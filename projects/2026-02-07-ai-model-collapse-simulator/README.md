## 💡 Why This Matters

Training models on their own synthetic outputs can silently degrade performance, create feedback loops, and amplify biases—phenomena the AI Model Collapse Simulator lets learners observe firsthand through interactive experiments. By simulating collapse scenarios, students build intuition about data provenance, evaluation pitfalls, and strategies like external validation or curated retraining that help preserve model reliability. This hands-on exploration prepares practitioners to design more robust ML workflows and to critically assess assumptions about data quality and autonomous systems.
