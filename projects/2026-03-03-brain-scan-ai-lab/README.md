## 💡 Why This Matters

BrainScan AI Lab gives learners a hands-on way to see how models infer biological sex from brain scans by combining practical methods, dataset confound analysis, interpretability exercises, and ethical experiment design. Exploring common confounds and interpretability tools helps distinguish genuine neural patterns from spurious correlations that models might exploit. With recent headlines about AI determining sex from brain scans, this lab trains learners to critically evaluate claims, reduce bias, and build more transparent, responsible AI for neuroscience.
