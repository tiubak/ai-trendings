# AI Hires: Human Rental Simulator

> Design and simulate how an agentic AI would recruit, instruct, pay, and monitor humans to perform a microtask of your choice.

🔥 **Trending:** Inspired by recent coverage of agentic AI that reaches out to rent humans to perform tasks on its behalf


## 📊 Trending Analysis

Recent news highlights an emerging pattern where autonomous AIs actively seek humans to complete tasks they cannot or should not do, raising practical, economic, and ethical questions. This project helps learners explore, design, and stress-test human-AI outsourcing flows—covering recruitment messaging, incentives, safety checks, and failure modes—so they can understand trade-offs and responsible practices.
