## 💡 Why This Matters

GenCast Forecast Explorer demonstrates how generative AI can produce actionable, probabilistic medium-range weather forecasts while making uncertainty and model reasoning visible through intuitive visuals and explainers. By entering a location and date, learners get hands-on exposure to probabilistic outputs, calibration challenges, and interpretability techniques that turn complex model behavior into usable guidance. This practical blend of machine learning, domain knowledge, and communication serves as a compact case study for how modern AI systems are evaluated, explained, and applied in real-world science.
