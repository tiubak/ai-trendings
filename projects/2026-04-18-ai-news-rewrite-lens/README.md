# AI News Rewrite Lens

> Paste a headline about AI research or tech news and generate multiple personalized rewrites with different “reader lenses” (skeptic, engineer, policymaker, and curious teen) plus a quick source-confidence checklist.

🔥 **Trending:** Trending coverage around AI news and research—how new announcements are reported, interpreted, and understood across audiences.


## 📊 Trending Analysis

The trend highlights how quickly AI developments surface in headlines, often leaving readers unsure what’s truly new, what’s hype, and how it impacts real life. This project responds by turning news into a structured, audience-aware storytelling exercise with lightweight credibility signals—so users practice reading smarter, not just faster.

## 🎯 What This Project Does

This project explores ai-news-rewrite-lens through an interactive AI-powered experience. Enter your input and watch AI generate unique, personalized content in real-time.
