## 💡 Why This Matters

AI Language Accessibility Lab lets users test prompts across different English proficiency and formality levels and receive rewrites, explanations, and tailored teaching exercises to reveal how LLMs perform for diverse speakers. Inspired by MIT Center for Constructive Communication research, the project uncovers performance gaps and turns research findings into practical tools that help developers, educators, and learners spot and fix misunderstandings. For someone learning about AI, this hands-on practice builds skills in prompt design, evaluation, and responsible deployment so models serve a wider range of users more reliably.
