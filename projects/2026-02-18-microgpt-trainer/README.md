## 💡 Why This Matters

Inspired by Karpathy's microGPT, MicroGPT Trainer lets learners train a tiny GPT model directly in the browser, watching weights update, loss decline, and generated text appear in real time. By exposing gradients, parameter updates, and sampling behavior, it turns abstract equations into hands-on intuition about how sequence models learn and fail. That immediate feedback and low barrier to experimentation accelerates learning, sharpens debugging skills, and helps learners move from conceptual understanding to practical model design with confidence.
