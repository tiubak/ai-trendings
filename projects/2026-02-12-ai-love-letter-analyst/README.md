## 💡 Why This Matters

Understanding how AI processes romantic language reveals how models handle nuance, metaphor, and emotional cues that appear across many NLP tasks. Building an AI Love Letter Analyst teaches practical techniques—sentiment analysis, style transfer, and personalization—while exposing challenges like ambiguity, cultural variation, and privacy. As a playful, hands-on project, it strengthens skills in dataset curation, model evaluation, and responsible design for systems that interact with human emotions.
