## 💡 Why This Matters

AI Alien Language Translator invents a fictional language and translates your messages while supplying grammar rules, a pronunciation guide, and cultural notes. By building and interacting with a system that creates coherent linguistic structures, learners observe how models detect patterns, generate rules, and balance creativity with internal consistency. The project provides a playful, hands-on way to practice prompt design, evaluate generative outputs, and probe model biases and limitations through iterative testing. Exploring this translator also surfaces broader questions about evaluation, explainability, and responsible design when AI shapes communication.
