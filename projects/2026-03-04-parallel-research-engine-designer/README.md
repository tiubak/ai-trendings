## 💡 Why This Matters

Inspired by recent cloud platforms offering multiple specialized research engines, the Parallel Research Engine Designer helps learners design and compare domain-specific AI engines with tailored architectures, training plans, cost estimates, and safety guidance. By making trade-offs explicit—between performance, compute budget, and risk—it turns abstract concepts into concrete design decisions. This matters for anyone learning AI because it teaches practical skills needed to evaluate feasibility, optimize resources, and embed safety from the start. Hands-on experimentation with realistic constraints accelerates readiness for research or industry roles.
